Anvil LUDO

Support information and privacy policy for the Android app.

Effective date: April 22, 2026

Support email: anvil.tech.support@gmail.com

Support

If you need help with Anvil LUDO, contact:

anvil.tech.support@gmail.com

Please include your device model, Android version, and a short issue description.

App Summary

  • Offline pass-and-play game for 2, 3, or 4 local players.
  • No account creation and no cloud save.
  • No gameplay history is stored.
  • One interstitial ad may appear after a match.
  • Optional daily reminder notification at 5:00 PM.

Privacy Policy

Anvil LUDO is designed to minimize data usage and keep gameplay local to the device.

  • We do not require sign-in or account registration.
  • We do not use Firebase services.
  • We do not store match history, profile data, or cloud backups.
  • We do not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or photos.
  • We store only a small local preference for terms acceptance and notification permission state.

Advertising

The app uses Google Mobile Ads to show one interstitial ad after a completed match.

  • Google Mobile Ads may process device and advertising identifiers according to Google's own policies.
  • Anvil LUDO does not sell personal information.
  • Anvil LUDO does not use Firebase Analytics in this build.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, the app can send one local reminder every day at 5:00 PM with a message similar to:

"Do not miss playing Anvil LUDO today!"

This reminder is scheduled on the device itself and is not sent from a server.

Contact

For support or policy questions, email anvil.tech.support@gmail.com.
